FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need money to start wholesaling?

No. Wholesaling does not require your own capital or your own credit. You get a property under contract, assign that contract to a cash buyer, and collect an assignment fee at closing. The buyer brings the money.

Do I need a real estate license?

Not to wholesale. You do need to structure your offers and contracts correctly to stay compliant in your state. The program includes the exact contract language and the LLC setup guidance for all 50 states.

How long until my first deal?

Our internal target is 90 days for students who follow the 1st Deal Challenge activity by activity. Some students close inside 3 weeks. Others take 4-5 months. Volume of offers is the biggest predictor.

What's the time commitment?

Most students run this alongside a full-time job. 8-12 hours per week is enough to hit the first-deal target if you follow the system. The training is designed for people who can't quit their day job yet.

Who is this NOT for?

Anyone expecting passive income or overnight results. Wholesaling is a real business. It takes reps, time on the phone, and following a system. If you want a get-rich-quick option, this isn't it.
